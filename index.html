Step 1

From the Airport to Tirana

Tirana International Airport "Mother Teresa" (TIA) is about 17 km from our apartment. The drive takes 25–40 minutes depending on traffic.

1

Collect Luggage & Exit the Terminal

After passport control, pick up your bags and head to the main arrivals exit. Turn left for car rental desks, or go straight for taxis and the bus.

2

Option A — Private Taxi

Taxis are right outside arrivals. Fare to city centre: 2,500–3,500 ALL (~€22–30). ~25–35 min.

📍 Taxi stand on Maps →
📱Taxi apps: Download "Speed Taxi" or "Merr Taxi" for fixed-price rides. Safer and transparent.
3

Option B — Car Rental

Several companies at the airport, from €10–20/day.

🚗Auto Rental Albania "ARA" — Near the terminal. Wide range, fast service.
📍 ARA on Maps →
🚐Minivans Rental Albania® — Vans & cars for groups/families.
📍 Minivans on Maps →
🏢Others: Rinas Car Rental, Karruka (4.9★, 1000+ reviews), Hertz, Avis, Budget, Sixt — desks in arrivals hall on the left.
4

Option C — Airport Bus (Budget)

Departs every hour to Tirana Central Station. Fare: ~400 ALL (~€3.50).

📍 Airport Bus Stop →
🏁From Central Station, apartment is ~15 min taxi or 25 min walk.
📍 Tirana Central Station →
5

Option D — Private Transfer

We can arrange a pickup (~€25–35). Message us on Airbnb in advance.

Step 2

Finding the Apartment

The building is on Rruga Tefta Tashko-Koço, next to Bar "Te Biblioteka" and City Public Library "Moikom Zeqo". Follow these photos step by step.

📍 Open GPS Location →
⚠️

Construction detour: Due to ongoing works near the building, follow the route on the map below. If arriving by car, you must follow the red line — Google Maps does not show the correct route in this area.

1

Follow the Red Line on the Map

This satellite view shows the correct route. The destination pin is at Bar "Te Biblioteka" — that's our building.

2

The Taxi Should Drop You Here

Ground floor has City Public Library "Moikom Zeqo" and coffee bar "Te Biblioteka". The red circle shows the apartment (3rd floor). Go behind the bar and up the stairs.

3

Follow the Path Behind the Building

Walk around to the back of the building following the red line. The stairs are located behind the building.

4

Go Up the Stairs

You'll see outdoor stairs with an orange handrail. Walk up to reach the building entrance. The apartment is on the 3rd floor.

5

Open the Lockbox & Enter

On the 3rd floor, open the lockbox (circled in red) with the code we'll send you, take the keys, and open the main door. You're home!

🅿️

Parking: The apartment doesn't include private parking. There's a paid parking lot right next to the building in the adjacent property.

Returning to the Airport

Plan ahead. The drive from city centre to TIA takes 25–40 minutes.

Plan Your Timing

Allow 2 hours before international flights, 1.5 hours domestic.

Check-out Checklist
